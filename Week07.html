Welcome to the Home Page. Imagine yourself standing in a quiet room.
In front of you lie three doors: the first is labeled "Contact," though no further context is given.
The second reads "Information," and looks mind-numbingly dull.
The third is adorned in jewels and gold inlay, and written in flowing silver script are the words "Form Submission."
Scattered about the room is a selection of furniature with very pedestrian taste, complete with tacky magazines.
There seems no point in staying here. Why not choose one of the doors?
